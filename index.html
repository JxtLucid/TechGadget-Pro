Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my TechGadget Pro? +
To reset your device, go to Settings > System > Reset Options. Choose between resetting settings or performing a full factory reset. Make sure to back up your data first.
What's the warranty period for TechGadget Pro devices? +
All TechGadget Pro devices come with a 24-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover accidental damage or normal wear and tear.
How can I improve my device's battery life? +
Try these tips: 1) Enable Battery Saver mode, 2) Reduce screen brightness, 3) Close unused apps running in the background, 4) Disable unnecessary location services, 5) Update to the latest software version.
Where can I find replacement parts and accessories? +
Genuine TechGadget Pro accessories and replacement parts are available through our official website and authorized retailers. We recommend using only genuine parts to ensure compatibility and safety.
